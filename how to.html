Use W A S and D to move the ship.

Use SPACE to fire. Firing depletes the blue energy bar. You cannot fire without energy.

Enemy bullets and crashing into enemies depletes health. Crashing into walls is instant death. If the red health bar is depleted, you lose.

Shoot red enemies to destroy them. Blue enemies are indestructible.

Your hit-box is limited to the center of the ship, but no part of you can collide with a wall.

Proceed all the way to the right to win.

There are no extra lives or continues.
